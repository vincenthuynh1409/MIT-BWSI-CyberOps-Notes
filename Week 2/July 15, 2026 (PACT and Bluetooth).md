<u>Bluetooth Basics:</u>
- **Transmit Parameters**
	- Low power (10 mW)
	- 2 ms out of every 270 ms
- **Receive Parameters:**
	- Requires waking the CPU, RAM, storage (1000 mW)
	- 4 sec out of 300 sec
- **Path Loss:**
	- Range Squared
	- Multipath Effects 4-10 dB variance
